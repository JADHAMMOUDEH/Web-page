Biography

Kylian Mbappe is a French professional footballer who plays as a forward for Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) and the French national team. He was born on December 20, 1998, in Bondy, France. Mbappe is known for his incredible speed, dribbling ability, and goal-scoring prowess.
